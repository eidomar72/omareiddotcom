BOOK REVIEWS

The Lost Symbol

Dan Brown

The thrill of following the symbologist Robert Langdon as he cracks codes, deciphers symbols, and unveils secrets hidden right in the heart of Washington, D.C. Brown's blend of art, history, and conspiracy theories creates a seriously captivating narrative that'll keep you turning pages. The plot moves at a brisk pace, immersing you in the mysterious realm of Freemasonry, ancient mysticism, and the hidden depths of American society.

And let's not forget that slick puzzle on the cover that'll make you feel like a code-cracking pro alongside Langdon.

The Da Vinci Code

Dan Brown

It's an electrifying rollercoaster of a thriller that throws you into deep rabbit-holes of art-historical puzzles. With everyone's favorite symbologist Robert Langdon steering this story you're plunged into a relentless chase through secret societies, religious history, and a whole bunch of conspiracy theories. Who doesn't love conspiracy theories...

Brown delivers a killer combo punch of suspend and mind-bending puzzles, leaving you breathless as you race to connect the dots before Langdon does.
